The Publishing Department Lesotho aims to provide open access to important documents, booklets, and announcements for public use.
The publishing department exists to share the gospel of hope and to make ready the people of God in a world fast approaching its end.
Under the denomination of IMSSDARM in Lesotho field, we translate and compile leaflets, booklets and other reading material designed
to spread the biblical truth, strengthen the faith, encourage and uplift.
Our materials are prayerfully crafted with the aim of pointing hearts to Christ and helping believers grow in their walk with God.
